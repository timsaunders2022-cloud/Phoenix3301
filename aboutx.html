The Story

Watch the Story of Cicada

Cicada 3301 was a mysterious organization that emerged in 2012, known for posting complex puzzles and riddles on the internet. The puzzles were designed to test the intelligence and creativity of those who attempted to solve them, and they quickly gained a cult following among puzzle enthusiasts and cryptographers.

After Cicada was disbanded, the remaining members went into hiding, but not before leaving behind a trail of cryptic messages and puzzles that would challenge the brightest minds for years to come.

One member, known only as "The Architect," continued to send messages and clues, leading to a global community of puzzle enthusiasts and cryptographers who dedicated themselves to uncovering the truth behind the organization.

As the years went by, the community grew larger and more dedicated, with some members even risking their lives to uncover the secrets of Cicada. The Architect continued to send messages, but they became increasingly difficult to decipher, leading many to believe that the organization had gone underground.

Despite the challenges, the community persisted, and eventually, they uncovered a hidden message that revealed the true purpose of Cicada: to create a new world order based on transparency, accountability, and justice.

Today, the legacy of Cicada lives on, with many members of the community continuing to work towards the organization's goals, even in the face of adversity. The story of Cicada serves as a reminder of the power of community and the importance of standing up for what you believe in, even when it seems impossible.
